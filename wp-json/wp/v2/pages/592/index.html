Hi, I’m Jaqueline!<\/h4>\n

I was born in S\u00e3o Paulo, Brazil.\u200b
\nThanks to a previous career in fashion I’ve been blessed with the opportunity of travelling and working all over the world.<\/p>\n

I lived throughout China, Thailand, Singapore, Germany, France, Czech Republic and Italy where I met my husband and spent five beautiful years of my life.
\n\u200b
\nI met wonderful people, discovered amazing places but mostly I absorbed ideas and inspiration for my burning passion: transforming beautiful dreams into beautiful spaces.<\/p>\n

By blending the vibrancy of my beloved Brazil, the simplicity of minimalism and the elegance of European art I create and design unique interiors for houses, museums, hotels, restaurants and every sort of space which needs a “soul”.
\n\u200b
\nI received my diploma after studying Interior Design at the National Design Academy while living in the UK for two years.<\/p>\n

\u200bOn top of my design skills I’m also proficient as a 3D Architectural Visualizer.<\/div>
